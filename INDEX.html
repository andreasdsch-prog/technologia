About This Showcase

Welcome to the Student HTML Project Showcase! This platform celebrates the creativity and technical skills of our coding students.

Here you'll find a diverse collection of web projects ranging from interactive games to practical web applications. Each project demonstrates fundamental HTML, CSS, and JavaScript concepts learned throughout our curriculum.

Students have the opportunity to showcase their work, receive feedback from peers, and gain inspiration from fellow developers. Browse through the gallery to see the amazing work our community has created!
